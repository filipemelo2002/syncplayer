Welcome to SyncPlayer

I started this project due to the lack of watching things sychronously on my computer along with somebody else, who wasn't physically with me. As the name suggests, this program syncs the "play" and "pause" buttons, allowing you to watch more confortably with somebody who is in another computer. This project is part of my studies in Electron, and I am not responsible for your misuse.
